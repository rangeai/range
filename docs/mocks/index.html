range · static mocks · v0

An agentic harness, not an IDE.

Range is the chat surface for robot sim work. Tell it what you want investigated, tuned, or verified. It picks the scenarios, runs the sweeps, reads the metrics, previews the outputs, and drafts the diff. You watch progress in a live tail pinned above the composer, and approve what matters.

five critical flows
01 · investigate
Diagnose a failing scenario.
Franka pick-place fails 3/10 in MuJoCo. Range sweeps 20 seeds with contact-depth instrumentation, isolates the failure cluster, proposes a solver-iteration bump.
priya · robotics dev · franka panda · mujoco
02 · tune
Sweep a controller across conditions.
Anymal-D stair climb · find best PID Kp across 4 slope angles × 4 mass perturbations × 25 seeds. Range fans out 400 runs, renders a heatmap, picks the sweet spot.
karthik · controls · anymal-d · mujoco
03 · regress
Check 8 baselines before merging.
Changes to the Warp contact solver. Range runs the baseline scenario suite against this branch and main, surfaces deltas, and traces the one regression.
anika · platform · warp · custom mjcf compiler
04 · author
Write a new scenario from a description.
"UR10 picks 5cm cubes off a conveyor at 0.2 m/s." Range writes scene.xml + task.py, runs a 10-trial smoke, previews the rollout video, and asks where to iterate.
priya · robotics dev · ur10 · mujoco
05 · reproduce
Reproduce a teammate's CI failure.
A walker gait test failed in CI at seed 42. Paste the job URL; Range pulls the failure, reproduces locally, bisects to the commit that introduced it, drafts a fix.
karthik · controls · walker · mujoco
Layout note. Every mock uses the same shell: a slim left rail with sessions, a header with the project, the conversation as the scrollable main, a live-activity bar pinned above the composer, and the composer pinned to the bottom of the viewport. Runs, sweeps, comparisons, artifacts, diffs, and approvals render as inline cards inside the conversation — never as separate panels.
