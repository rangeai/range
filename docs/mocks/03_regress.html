Good news first: cloth scenarios are clearly better. Bunny goes from 70 → 100% and is also faster. The jitter fix is real.

But locomotion.stair_climb regressed from 92% → 78%. Looking at the failing rollouts: in 14 of the new failures, the rear foot penetrates the riser by ~3mm before the solver corrects. Your looser epsilon is letting the foot penetrate further before the contact constraint kicks in, and the swing-leg controller compensates by overshooting.

I bisected the epsilon to find the worst threshold for stair_climb:
